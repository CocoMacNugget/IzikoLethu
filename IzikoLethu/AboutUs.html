About us

We help students find accommodation near their campus. Search through available properties, view photos and details, and submit applications directly to landlords. All properties are verified and landlords are vetted. You can save your favorite listings and communicate with property owners all in one place.

We work with landlords in multiple cities to bring you quality accommodation options. Each listing includes photos, pricing, and amenities so you know exactly what you're getting. Create an account to save properties you like and keep track of your applications. Our support team is here to help if you have questions about any listing.
